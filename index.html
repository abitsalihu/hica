Our story -

The Huron Indian Cultural Association (HICA) was founded in 2018 to celebrate, enjoy, and share Indian culture with the Huron community.

At HICA, our goal is to support Indian students by creating a tight-knit Indian community. Moreover, we want to create a large family at Huron, which promotes diversity and individuality.

HICA strives to educate and share Indian culture, including traditions, festivities, attire, and food amongst students interested in expanding their cultural knowledge.

HICA is the perfect place to get a taste of the vibrant Indian culture!
